👋 About Me

I am Chandan Kumar, serving as Sr. Manager (Environment & Safety) at Central Coalfields Limited (CCL), Kathara Area, Jharkhand. With a deep commitment to environmental stewardship and community well-being, I have been actively coordinating CCL's Swachhata Pakhwada campaign since its inception as a flagship national cleanliness initiative.

My work spans the full lifecycle of environmental compliance and community engagement — from managing environmental impact assessments at colliery sites to designing grassroots sanitation campaigns that reach schools, villages, PHCs, and Anganwadis across the mining belt of Jharkhand.

This portal was conceived, planned, and built as a one-stop digital governance tool for Swachhata Pakhwada 2026, enabling real-time tracking of activities across 14 CCL areas, transparent impact reporting, and sustained community engagement well beyond the campaign's 15-day window.

🖥️ About This Portal

The Swachhata Pakhwada 2026 portal was built entirely as a static HTML/CSS/JavaScript website — hosted for free on GitHub Pages with zero backend maintenance. Every page is hand-coded to ensure fast load times even on mobile data connections in the field.
